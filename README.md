# LinkBio — The Link-in-Bio Page Builder

Your own customizable page that lives behind one link — for everything you want the internet to know about you.

## What it is

LinkBio is a self-hosted alternative to the familiar "link in bio" pages. Instead of handing over your audience to a third party, LinkBio lets anyone create their own branded mini-page that holds every important link in one place — portfolio, shop, latest post, newsletter, booking calendar, contact form.

Creators sign up, design their page with drag-and-drop ease, pick a handle, and share a single URL everywhere — social bios, email signatures, business cards. Visitors land on something that looks like the creator, not like a template.

## Who it's for

- **Creators and influencers** pushing followers from short-bio social platforms to their wider work.
- **Small businesses and freelancers** consolidating booking pages, menus, catalogs, and contact forms.
- **Artists and musicians** linking to streaming platforms, merch stores, and upcoming shows.
- **Writers, podcasters, and newsletter authors** pointing to every platform their work lives on.
- **Job seekers and students** building a lightweight personal landing page.

## Use cases

- Replace the clutter of a single bio link with a beautiful page of everything you do.
- Promote a launch, event, or product drop with a temporary top link.
- Collect emails, inquiries, or bookings directly from the page.
- Track which links your audience clicks and adjust accordingly.
- Offer a consistent landing experience across every platform and campaign.

## Core capabilities

- **Account system** — sign up, log in, and manage your page privately.
- **Dashboard** for adding, editing, reordering, and hiding links.
- **Public profile page** at a clean shareable URL.
- **Branded design** with customization to match the creator's identity.
- **Informational pages** (About, FAQ, Resources, Contact) so creators understand the tool and can reach out for help.

## The idea in one line

LinkBio is your entire online presence, folded neatly into one tap — and owned by you, not a platform.
